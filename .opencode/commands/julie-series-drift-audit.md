# /julie-series-drift-audit

## Purpose

Audit a book or the whole series for long-form drift: canon bloat, repeated threat mechanics, Julie arc flattening, institutional consequence resets, and commercial-series fatigue.

## Usage

```text
/julie-series-drift-audit <book-number-or-series>
```

## Required Skills

- `julie-series-canon`
- `julie-series-memory`
- `julie-continuity-auditor`
- `julie-ai-plausibility`
- `julie-publication-readiness`

## Task

1. Verify repo scope.
2. Read series bible and relevant book files.
3. Compare current book against prior series patterns.
4. Identify drift, repetition, escalation problems, and unresolved-thread neglect.
5. Recommend canon repairs or book-level adjustments.
6. Do not rewrite manuscript unless explicitly requested.

## Drift Checklist

- Julie is not becoming too healed too quickly.
- AI threats are not repeating too closely.
- ARGUS/Argus canon is stable.
- Institutional consequences do not disappear.
- Supporting cast relationships evolve.
- Villains remain human and plausible.
- The series arc advances.
- Technical realism remains grounded.
- Reader promise stays clear.

## Output Destination

```text
series-bible/series-drift-audits/YYYY-MM-DD-drift-audit.md
```

If the folder does not exist, create it.
