# Skill: Julie Publication Readiness

## Purpose

Assess Julie O'Donnell manuscripts and publication assets for release quality across formatting, prose, continuity, metadata, reader promise, and commercial thriller expectations.

Use this skill at chapter-level pre-lock, full-book pre-export, and platform metadata review.

## Chapter-Level Checks

- Clean title and heading format.
- No outline fragments or internal notes left in prose.
- Scene starts and ends cleanly.
- No unresolved continuity conflict introduced.
- No unintentional POV drift.
- No formatting artifacts.
- No repeated AI-ish phrasing.
- Chapter advances plot and mystery.

## Full-Book Checks

- Opening hook works quickly.
- Julie's arc is emotionally coherent.
- Technical mechanism is understandable and plausible.
- Villain/motive reveal is earned.
- Clue map aligns with final reveal.
- Red herrings are fair and useful.
- Timeline is coherent.
- Ending resolves immediate crisis but preserves series engine.
- No dangling accidental threads.
- Front/back matter is complete.
- Metadata matches book contents.

## Platform Checks

For KDP/Draft2Digital:

- title and subtitle consistency;
- author/pen name consistency;
- series name and volume number;
- short description;
- long description;
- categories;
- keywords;
- content warning notes if needed;
- ebook formatting readiness;
- print trim assumptions if applicable;
- audiobook notes if applicable.

## Output Requirements

Use severity levels:

- **Critical** — must fix before publication.
- **Recommended** — should fix before publication.
- **Optional** — polish or marketing preference.

Always end with:

- publication readiness rating;
- blocker list;
- next recommended command.
