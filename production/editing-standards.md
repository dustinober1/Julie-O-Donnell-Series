# Editing Standards

## Purpose

Define what a finished Julie O'Donnell chapter should satisfy before it is considered revised.

## Standards

- Scene has clear pressure and turn.
- Dialogue carries subtext and conflict.
- Julie remains competent and emotionally restrained.
- Technical explanation is clear but not lecture-like.
- No major canon drift.
- No repeated AI-ish phrases.
- Chapter ending changes the story state.

## Revision Priorities

1. Canon and clue integrity.
2. Tension and pacing.
3. Character pressure.
4. Dialogue texture.
5. Prose freshness.
6. Formatting cleanup.
