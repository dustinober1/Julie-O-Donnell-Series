# Back Cover Blurb Template

## Hook Line

A single high-concept line that frames the threat.

## Setup Paragraph

Introduce Julie, her current state, and the inciting crisis.

## Escalation Paragraph

Show the system threat, institutional pressure, and why the official explanation is dangerous.

## Personal Stakes Paragraph

Connect the case to Julie's old wound and the human cost.

## Final Sales Line

End with a sharp thriller promise.

## Guardrails

- Do not overexplain the technical mechanism.
- Do not reveal the final villain.
- Do not make the book sound like science fiction if it is grounded contemporary thriller.
- Do not promise a romance subplot unless it exists.
- Do not mislabel Julie as currently military if she is former military.
