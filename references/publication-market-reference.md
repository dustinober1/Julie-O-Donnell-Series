# Publication Market Reference

## Purpose

Collect market, positioning, metadata, cover, blurb, and launch research for the Julie O'Donnell series.

## Use This File For

- Comparable authors and titles.
- Category and keyword research.
- Cover trends.
- Blurb hooks.
- Series sell-through strategy.
- Audiobook market notes.
- Advertising angles.

## Positioning Guardrail

The series should be marketed as a grounded contemporary intelligence / techno-thriller, not as cyberpunk or general AI philosophy.

## Research Needed

- Current Amazon thriller categories.
- Techno-thriller cover patterns.
- AI thriller comp titles.
- Kindle Unlimited versus wide strategy.
- Audiobook narrator positioning.
- Newsletter and ad hooks.

## Notes Rule

When adding market research, include date accessed and source. Market facts change quickly.
