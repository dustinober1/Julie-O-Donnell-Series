# Medical AI Reference

## Purpose

Support future Julie O'Donnell books involving hospital triage, medical risk scoring, allocation systems, health-data analytics, or care-denial algorithms.

## Use This File For

- Risk scores.
- Triage priority.
- Proxy variables.
- Resource allocation.
- Hospital administration pressure.
- Clinical decision support systems.
- Appeal and override pathways.

## Possible Thriller Engine

A system appears to optimize care but quietly deprioritizes a group of patients because the objective function rewards efficiency, liability reduction, or resource protection.

## Human Stakes

Medical AI stories should center patients, families, clinicians under pressure, and administrators protecting a record.

## Research Needed

- Clinical decision support limitations.
- Risk scoring criticism and bias.
- Hospital triage workflow.
- Appeals and human override in clinical systems.
