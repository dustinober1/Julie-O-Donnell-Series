# Predictive Policing Reference

## Purpose

Support future books involving policing analytics, threat scoring, patrol allocation, watchlists, or domestic-security automation.

## Use This File For

- Feedback-loop escalation.
- Patrol allocation bias.
- Watchlist inertia.
- Human review failure.
- Risk score overreliance.
- Official record pressure.

## Possible Thriller Engine

A risk system sends more enforcement toward places or people already over-measured, creating new data that falsely confirms the original risk score.

## Human Stakes

Keep the focus on people misclassified by the system, investigators pressured to trust it, and officials who defend it because admitting error undermines past actions.

## Guardrail

Avoid simplistic politics. The strongest story pressure comes from real safety concerns colliding with bad evidence and institutional fear.
