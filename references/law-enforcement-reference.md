# Law Enforcement Reference

## Purpose

Collect notes for future books involving law enforcement, predictive policing, threat scoring, investigations, warrants, task forces, or public-safety analytics.

## Use This File For

- Investigative procedure.
- Multiagency coordination.
- Watchlist and threat assessment story logic.
- Predictive policing failure modes.
- Chain of custody.
- Search warrants and evidence handling at a high fictional level.

## Fiction Guardrail

Keep procedure plausible but readable. Do not let procedural detail stall thriller pacing.

## Future Book Use

This reference is especially relevant if a later book focuses on predictive policing, domestic threat scoring, or automated watchlist escalation.
