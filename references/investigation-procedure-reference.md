# Investigation Procedure Reference

## Purpose

Track reusable investigative structure for Julie O'Donnell books.

## Use This File For

- Interview sequencing.
- Evidence narrowing.
- Document review.
- Log analysis.
- Witness contradiction.
- Timeline reconstruction.
- Institutional access barriers.
- How proof becomes usable under pressure.

## Julie Investigation Pattern

Julie should move from anomaly to pattern to mechanism to motive. She may suspect early, but she must earn proof.

## Evidence Types

- data outputs;
- source packets;
- provenance metadata;
- emails or memos;
- logs and missing logs;
- witness memory;
- public record language;
- procurement or contractor records;
- crisis-room decisions.

## Guardrail

Do not confuse suspicion with proof. Thriller momentum improves when Julie has to make a dangerous next move because proof is incomplete.
