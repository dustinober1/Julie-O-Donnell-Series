# Book 1 Character Arcs

## Julie O'Donnell

### Starting State

Julie is in practical exile from government systems, living with distance from the institution that broke her record.

### Pressure

A new ARGUS-family crisis forces her to reengage under supervision.

### Midpoint Movement

Julie must move from private recognition to shareable proof.

### Final Movement

Julie helps restore uncertainty and pause escalation, but the official record remains only partially corrected.

## Mara Keene

- Arc should test whether she protects process or truth.

## Evan Rusk

## Dr. Selene Cho

## Hargrove

## Reed Calder

## Vance
