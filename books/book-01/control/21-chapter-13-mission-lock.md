## Abort condition

**Hard abort trigger:** At **07:42:30 EDT**, the operation aborts unless both of the following are true:

1. Julie has established continuous observation, or a defensibly recoverable observation record, of the suspected SSO-NS-004 carrier stream, access lane, support vehicle, or staff/courier route into Hartwell.
2. Marcus has a viable exfiltration route with the primary evidence, and Elias can confirm that the incident module and administrator-token board remain in his custody and usable.

If either condition is false at 07:42:30 EDT, the trio must stop pressing the Hartwell perimeter and fall back to evidence preservation and independent custody/witness strategy.

**Immediate abort triggers before 07:42:30 EDT:**

- The aluminum case, recovery cartridge, custody log, or dead transponder is lost, seized, or separated from Marcus without a documented successor custody path.
- The incident module or administrator-token board is lost, seized, physically damaged, or separated from Elias without a documented successor custody path.
