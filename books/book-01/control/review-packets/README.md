# Book 1 Specialist Review Packets

These packets are designed for external reviewers of *Veridrift*. They define the exact review scope and prevent an expert correction from expanding into an untracked story rewrite.

## Canonical review source

Review only the prologue and chapter files listed in `../../ACCEPTED_MANUSCRIPT.yaml`. Generated compilations may be supplied for convenience, but the accepted inventory controls any disagreement.

## Instructions for every reviewer

- State your name, qualifications, and review date.
- Identify the exact accepted-manifest commit or compilation reviewed.
- Rank each finding as **critical**, **material**, **minor**, or **preference**.
- Quote or identify only enough of the affected passage to locate it.
- Recommend the smallest correction that preserves plot, timing, character decisions, suspense, and the ending.
- Separate field accuracy from stylistic preference.
- Confirm which mechanisms are credible and should not be overcorrected.
- Do not infer a human operator, motive, command, or physical possession when the source establishes only a registered authority or system event.
- End with **approved**, **approved with corrections**, or **not approved**.

## Severity definitions

- **Critical:** A knowledgeable reader would reject the central mechanism, law, safety behavior, causal chain, or institutional action; publication must stop until fixed.
- **Material:** The scene remains understandable but is likely to damage credibility with informed readers.
- **Minor:** Terminology, sequencing, or small procedural detail that can be corrected during copyedit.
- **Preference:** A stylistic or policy choice with more than one defensible real-world implementation.

## Editorial handling

External reviewers establish accuracy ceilings, not story ownership. Findings are entered into `../53-specialist-findings-ledger.md`, adjudicated, applied on a review branch, and followed by a full continuity pass. No specialist area is complete merely because a packet was sent or an informal conversation occurred.
