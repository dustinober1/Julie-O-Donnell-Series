## Core rule

Authority is domain-specific. A person or institution may control a room, system, source, investigation, scene, device, or decision without owning every other domain.

## Authority-continuity rules

1. Sponsorship is not receiving custody.
2. Facility authority is not criminal investigative authority.
3. Classification ownership is not physical evidence custody.
4. Source custody is not merits adoption.
5. Technical relevance does not expand consent or jurisdiction.
6. Police scene control does not validate intelligence claims.
7. Federal receiving responsibility does not transfer foreign or legislative originals automatically.
8. Every scope expansion must identify the person or institution that owns it.
