# Book 1 Continuity Ledgers

These ledgers sit beneath `../README.md` and `../../ACCEPTED_MANUSCRIPT.yaml`. They do not replace the accepted prose. They expose the facts that must remain synchronized whenever a specialist correction, compression edit, copyedit, or production correction changes the manuscript.

## Ledger set

1. `01-master-timeline.md` — clocks, dates, scene order, and cross-time-zone conversions.
2. `02-evidence-custody-ledger.md` — originals, derivatives, seals, custodians, openings, and transfers.
3. `03-injury-ledger.md` — injury acquisition, symptoms, capability ceilings, treatment, and disposition.
4. `04-knowledge-ledger.md` — what each principal knows, does not know, infers, and can prove.
5. `05-authority-ledger.md` — command, facility, program, police, investigative, court, and source-custody authority.
6. `06-technology-ledger.md` — exact capabilities and proof limits of the story's systems and devices.
7. `07-public-narrative-ledger.md` — the changing official account, its source, and the corrections that follow.

## Use rule

Every accepted critical or material specialist correction must identify which ledgers it affects. The ledgers are then updated before prose changes are considered complete.

## Proof-language rule

Keep these categories separate:

- registered authority;
- institutional custody;
- physical possession;
- physical operation;
- personal authentication;
- command or instruction;
- motive or intent.

A lower category never silently proves a higher one.

## Status markers

- **LOCKED:** Controlling story fact; may change only if the content lock is deliberately reopened.
- **VALIDATE:** Story fact is canon but the mechanism or terminology requires external specialist review.
- **OPEN:** Deliberately unresolved within Book 1.
- **DERIVED:** Inference stated by a character; not independently established.

## Publication gate

After technical corrections, every ledger must be reviewed against all 25 accepted prose files before line/copyedit begins. After copyedit, the validator and a manual ledger audit must both pass before production export.
