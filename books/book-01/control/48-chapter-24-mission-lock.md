- **Final image function:** the farm's honest physical work remains, including a fence or tool her right hand cannot manage. Julie neither forces the repair alone nor treats incapacity as defeat. She accepts bounded help and places the written terms of future work beside the physical work waiting for her.
- **Exit:** Book 1 ends on chosen structure, agency, and shared burden—not on a new case.
