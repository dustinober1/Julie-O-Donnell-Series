## Expansion and compression controls

### Expand when

- the named-receiver distinction is not dramatized;
- Marcus’s correction lacks exact wording or cost;
- Hackett’s choice remains vague;
- Sharma’s acknowledgment appears without an institutional source;
- medical authority does not constrain the statement; or
- the endpoint does not materially strengthen preservation.

### Compress when

- package contents are inventoried again after identifiers and seals are established;
- travel does not alter custody or medical authority;
- characters repeat already-established evidence limits;
- injuries are restated without changing an action;
- institutional titles are listed without creating responsibility;
- the allied record is explained beyond what the preservation request requires; or
- later-chapter possibilities begin to form an outline.
