- characters repeat already-established evidence;
- another chase reproduces an earlier escape without new consequence;
- technical explanation does not alter a decision;
- an antagonist scene says what the evidence already shows;
- a series seed competes with Book 1 closure; or
- a chapter has more than one dominant mission.
