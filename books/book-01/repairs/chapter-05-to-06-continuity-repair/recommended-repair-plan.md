The 16:30 event becomes allied source certification. The 05:00 event remains the executable support commit. They make one actual infiltration at 04:31, recover Elias, descend through the incident-transfer route, and trigger Shutter L3-7 at 04:51–04:52.

**Sections changed:** Targeted deadline references in Chapters 1–5; Chapter 5 title; Chapter 5 from the 15:49 utility-easement heading through its ending.

**Preserves:** The farm departure, daytime Apex review, motel, library, shopping, initial escape, stormwater method, Elias detention, Vance's pressure, Compliance Four rescue, ethical debate, evidence case, and all Chapters 6–12.

**New explanation required:** The warning and the executable support product are separate authenticated stages. The flooded approach delays the same reentry operation without creating an unexplained hiding period inside Apex.

**India-side effect:** A new 02:00 IST source-certification beat shows readiness escalation. The accepted 14:29–14:30 IST firing decision remains unchanged.

**Effect on Chapters 6–12:** None. Chapter 6's heading, timestamp, first line, injuries, and subsequent events remain intact.

**Pacing cost:** Moderate. The overnight watch creates compression rather than rest and makes the second clock explicit.

**Continuity risk:** Lowest of the available approaches.
