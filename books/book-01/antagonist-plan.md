# Book 1 Antagonist Plan

## Antagonist Structure

Book 1 should use layered antagonism rather than a single cartoon villain.

## Reed Calder

- Function: primary human exploiter/data broker.
- Connection: old Argus Beta field-trial link and present-day vendor-provenance role.
- Threat: understands how trusted pipelines can manufacture certainty.
- Needs resolution: exact prior and current titles.

## Vance

- Function: political containment pressure.
- Motive: protect oversight optics, appropriations pressure, and ARGUS reputation.
- Threat: makes uncertainty politically inconvenient.
- Needs resolution: exact title and authority.

## Dr. Selene Cho

- Function: Apex Defense technical/executive face.
- Motive: defend system credibility while privately recognizing vulnerability may be real.
- Threat: can block or slow technical truth.
- Needs resolution: exact Apex title and ethical boundary.

## Hargrove

- Function: prologue command-pressure figure and later complicated witness.
- Motive: protect record, authority, or self-concept.
- Threat: embodies the original institutional pressure.

## Reveal Rule

The final solution must connect the human motive to the technical mechanism. Avoid confession monologues.
