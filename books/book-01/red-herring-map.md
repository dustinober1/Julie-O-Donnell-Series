# Book 1 Red Herring Map

## Purpose

Track false leads that are plausible, useful, and fair.

## Red Herring Rules

A red herring should teach something true even if it is not the final answer.

## Candidate Red Herrings

### Foreign State Direct Control

- Surface theory: Pakistan-linked actors directly controlled or fabricated the classification evidence.
- Useful truth: foreign-state ambiguity creates real pressure.
- Final function: the system's confidence is still wrong, but not because of a simple direct-state-control answer.

### Apex Engineering Error

- Surface theory: ARGUS-ENT-4.6 has a normal model defect or drift problem.
- Useful truth: Apex has real governance and design failures.
- Final function: the key mechanism is exploited trusted provenance, not accidental drift alone.

### Julie's Trauma Bias

- Surface theory: Julie is projecting Argus Beta onto a new crisis.
- Useful truth: her trauma complicates trust and credibility.
- Final function: Julie still needs proof, but her pattern recognition is grounded.

### Hargrove as Mastermind

- Surface theory: Hargrove covered up the old incident and may be driving the new one.
- Useful truth: command pressure and official record manipulation matter.
- Final function: Hargrove is a complicated witness/pressure figure, not necessarily the primary exploiter.

## Watchpoint

Do not let red herrings become filler. Each must move the reader closer to the final mechanism.
