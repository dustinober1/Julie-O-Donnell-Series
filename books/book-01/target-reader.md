# Book 1 Target Reader

## Primary Reader

Readers of contemporary intelligence, military, political, and techno-thrillers who like competent protagonists, institutional pressure, and plausible high-stakes systems.

## Reader Wants

- A damaged but highly capable protagonist.
- Smart clue logic.
- Government and contractor pressure.
- AI/system threat grounded in reality.
- Moral stakes tied to real people.
- Escalation without cartoon villainy.

## Reader Does Not Want

- Dense AI lectures.
- Magic hacking.
- Sentient AI as the culprit.
- Overly abstract political arguments.
- A protagonist who is either helpless or invincible.

## Book 1 Delivery Standard

The book should be accessible to general thriller readers while credible enough that technically informed readers do not reject the mechanism.
