# Book 1 Premise and Hook

## Core Hook

What if the system was wrong — and everyone in power needed it to be right?

## One-Sentence Premise

Former Army intelligence officer Julie O'Donnell is pulled back into the ARGUS family of systems after a convoy attack near the Line of Control triggers a warning-classification cascade that may turn engineered certainty into geopolitical escalation.

## Expanded Premise

Six years earlier, Julie warned that Argus Beta was being fed a pattern too perfect to be real. Command overrode her, the official record buried the warning, and Julie left the Army with her credibility broken. Now ARGUS-ENT-4.6 is producing a new certainty in a new crisis, and Julie must prove the system is being manipulated before decision-makers act on a lie.

## Commercial Promise

A contemporary intelligence techno-thriller about AI confidence, government pressure, contractor opacity, and one damaged analyst who knows certainty can be manufactured.
