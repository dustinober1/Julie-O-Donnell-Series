# Prologue Blueprint

## Status

Needs planning pass.

## Locked Purpose

Show Julie six years ago at a forward operating base near the Pakistan border identifying engineered certainty in Argus Beta under command pressure.

## Required Elements

- Julie as competent Army intelligence officer.
- Argus Beta output appears too perfect.
- Command pressure escalates.
- Julie warns the data stream is engineered.
- The scene plants the wound that later becomes official-record damage.

## Forbidden Moves

- Do not reveal the full Book 1 present-day mechanism.
- Do not make Argus Beta sentient.
- Do not make Julie merely afraid; she is analytically right.
