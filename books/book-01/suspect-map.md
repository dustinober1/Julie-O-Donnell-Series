# Book 1 Suspect Map

## Purpose

Track suspect logic, motive, access, and reveal timing.

## Reed Calder

- Status: primary exploiter candidate.
- Motive: TBD; likely profit, leverage, revenge, protection, or learned contempt for institutional trust.
- Access: vendor-provenance role and old Argus Beta connection.
- Reveal timing: late middle to final act.

## Vance

- Status: institutional containment antagonist.
- Motive: protect political optics, appropriations, crisis posture, and ARGUS credibility.
- Access: influence over official record and Julie's access.
- Reveal timing: present from early act, but full pressure escalates later.

## Dr. Selene Cho

- Status: technical defender / possible ambiguous antagonist.
- Motive: protect Apex, protect system reputation, avoid liability, preserve technical belief.
- Access: deep technical knowledge.
- Reveal timing: should complicate investigation, not simply reveal villainy.

## Hargrove

- Status: old command-pressure figure and complicated witness.
- Motive: protect old record, reputation, or command decisions.
- Access: old incident memory and possible present influence.
- Reveal timing: Act III witness role.

## Unknown External Actors

- Status: early theory / crisis pressure.
- Motive: regional destabilization.
- Access: uncertain.
- Function: create plausible official explanation without being the final simple answer.

## Suspect Rule

Every suspect should have motive, access, and pressure — but not every suspect should have all three in the same way.
