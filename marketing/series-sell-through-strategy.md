## Series Goal

Design each book so readers want the next Julie O'Donnell case because the immediate case resolves but the larger ARGUS/institutional record problem deepens.

## Sell-Through Levers

- Clear series branding.
- Distinct book-level AI/system threat.
- Julie's long emotional arc.
- Recurring cast consequences.
- Unresolved institutional threads.
- Back matter that points to the next book.

## Book Ending Rule

Resolve the immediate thriller crisis. Carry forward a meaningful relationship, institutional, or ARGUS-family consequence.
