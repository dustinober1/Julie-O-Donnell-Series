# Reader Avatar

## Primary Reader

A thriller reader who likes intelligence operations, political pressure, competent damaged protagonists, and plausible modern technology threats.

## Wants

- Fast but intelligent pacing.
- High stakes.
- Moral pressure.
- Realistic systems.
- Institutional conflict.
- A protagonist with scars and skills.

## Avoids

- Dense technical lectures.
- Cartoon villains.
- Evil AI clichés.
- Slow bureaucracy without suspense.
