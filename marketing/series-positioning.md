# Series Positioning

## Positioning Statement

A grounded contemporary intelligence thriller series about a former Army intelligence officer who exposes how human motives hide inside machine certainty.

## Core Hook

What if the system was wrong — and everyone in power needed it to be right?

## Genre Lane

- Intelligence thriller
- Military techno-thriller
- Political thriller
- AI/system thriller

## Differentiator

Julie O'Donnell is not fighting evil AI. She is fighting institutions that use AI confidence as permission.
