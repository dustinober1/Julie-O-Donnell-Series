# Audiobook Strategy

## Status

Placeholder pending manuscript and narrator plan.

## Direction

The series could work well in audiobook if narrated with grounded intelligence-thriller restraint rather than exaggerated action delivery.

## Voice Priorities

- Julie: controlled, intelligent, guarded.
- Institutional characters: polished pressure.
- Technical scenes: clear, natural, not lecture-like.
- Crisis scenes: tense without melodrama.

## Future Decisions

- Single narrator versus multi-voice.
- ACX exclusive versus wide audio.
- Narrator availability for long-running series.
- Release timing relative to ebook launch.
