# Ad Hooks

## Current Hook Bank

- What if the system was wrong — and everyone in power needed it to be right?
- The machine did not lie. Someone taught it what to trust.
- Certainty is the most dangerous weapon in the room.
- She warned them once. They buried the record. Now the system is back.
- The official record says she hesitated. The system says it is certain. Both may be lies.
