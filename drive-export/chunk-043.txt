07:08 EDT.

A later operational exchange connected to K-17, scheduled through Northbridge.

“Yes.”

Then he looked at Julie.

“Who do we trust?”

Julie looked at the sealed case, Elias’s bandaged hand, and the blood beginning to show through the edge of Marcus’s dressing.

“No one yet.”
