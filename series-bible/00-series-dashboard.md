## Core Promise

A grounded, high-stakes thriller series about a former Army intelligence officer who recognizes when automated systems convert uncertainty into deadly certainty, and who must expose the human incentives hiding behind machine authority.

## Current Book 1 Foundation

- Opening wound: Argus Beta incident at a forward operating base near the Pakistan border, six years earlier.
- Present crisis: ARGUS-ENT-4.6 / RoboWarning risks classifying a Line of Control convoy attack as Pakistan-linked state sponsorship.
- Core mechanism: trusted data/provenance manipulation that inflates confidence through self-corroboration.
- Emotional engine: Julie must return to the family of systems that destroyed her credibility.

## Current Locked Facts

- Julie is a former U.S. Army Captain and Army intelligence officer.
- Argus Beta was the old system involved in the prologue-era failure.
- ARGUS-ENT-4.6 is the present enterprise platform in Book 1.
- RoboWarning is the warning-classification module in Book 1.
- ARGUS is not sentient.
- Book 1 is about human exploitation of trusted systems, not magic AI.
