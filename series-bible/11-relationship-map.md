# Relationship Map

## Purpose

Track the emotional, professional, and institutional relationships that shape Julie O'Donnell's choices across the series.

## Julie and Mara Keene

- Current function: Mara is the old colleague/access controller who pulls Julie back in under supervised conditions.
- Tension: Mara may believe Julie but still represent the system Julie distrusts.
- Relationship engine: trust under constraint.
- Watchpoint: do not make Mara instantly loyal without cost.

## Julie and Evan Rusk

- Current function: former prologue-era analyst/internal skeptic.
- Tension: shared memory of Argus Beta, possible guilt, doubt, or compromised record.
- Relationship engine: what people knew then versus what they are willing to say now.
- Watchpoint: avoid making Evan a pure exposition device.

## Julie and Hargrove

- Current function: prologue command-pressure figure; later complicated witness.
- Tension: authority, memory, blame, survival, official record.
- Relationship engine: confrontation with the person who embodied command pressure.
- Watchpoint: avoid cartoon villainy.

## Julie and Vance

- Current function: political containment pressure.
- Tension: public stability versus truth.
- Relationship engine: Julie's evidence threatens the official action path.
- Watchpoint: give Vance plausible institutional reasons.

## Julie and Dr. Selene Cho

- Current function: Apex technical/executive face.
- Tension: engineering reality versus corporate defense.
- Relationship engine: one expert recognizes another but cannot admit the same thing publicly.
- Watchpoint: do not make Cho either purely evil or instantly converted.

## Julie and Reed Calder

- Current function: human exploiter/data broker.
- Tension: old Argus Beta connections and present vendor-provenance manipulation.
- Relationship engine: someone learned how institutions trust machine certainty.
- Watchpoint: motive must remain human and grounded.

## Update Rule

After each chapter that changes a relationship, update the active book's `character-arcs.md` first. Promote to this file only if it affects future books.
