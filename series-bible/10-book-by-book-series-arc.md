# Book-by-Book Series Arc

## Purpose

Track the long-range Julie O'Donnell series plan so each book feels fresh while advancing the central ARGUS/institutional reckoning.

## Series Arc Principle

Julie begins as a person whose warning was erased from the record. Across the series, she should move toward forcing a public record that institutions cannot bury.

## Book 1 — Untitled

### Status

### Core Threat

National-security warning classification and ARGUS-family provenance manipulation.

### Failure Mode

Provenance laundering, self-corroboration, confidence inflation, whitelisted integration abuse.

### Julie Movement

From exile/avoidance to reluctant reentry and the beginning of a corrected record.

### Ending Function

Immediate escalation is paused by restoring uncertainty. Institutional truth remains incomplete.
