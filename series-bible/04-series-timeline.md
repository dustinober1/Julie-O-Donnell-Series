# Series Timeline

## Purpose

Maintain chronological continuity across Julie O'Donnell books.

## Timeline Rules

- Use absolute dates once canonized.
- Mark uncertain timing as provisional.
- Do not compress travel, investigation, or institutional process unrealistically unless the story provides a reason.
- Keep public events, hearings, military incidents, and case consequences consistent.

## Locked Timeline

### Six Years Before Book 1 Present Day

- Julie O'Donnell serves as a U.S. Army Captain / intelligence officer at a forward operating base near the Pakistan border.
- Argus Beta provides AI-enabled SIGINT/targeting confidence outputs.
- Julie identifies a pattern that is too perfect and likely engineered.
- Command pressure overrides her warning.
- The incident damages or ends her Army career.
- Later oversight/hearing processes reframe her warning as officer hesitation.

## Book 1 Present Day

- Julie is living away from government systems, provisionally in Culpeper, Virginia.
- A joint logistics convoy attack near the Line of Control creates a regional crisis.
- ARGUS-ENT-4.6 / RoboWarning classifies the event in a way that may support escalation.
- Julie is pulled back under supervised conditions by Mara Keene.
